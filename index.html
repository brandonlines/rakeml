How the model works

A simplified path from challenge framing to autonomous experimentation and translation into real-world applications.

Step 1

Frame priority challenges

Teams define performance targets in health, energy, electronics, transportation, and green products.

Step 2

Run self-driving lab cycles

AI, robotics, and advanced computation execute rapid design-make-test loops with reproducible data pipelines.

Step 3

Scale with consortium partners

Results move into commercialization pathways through cross-sector collaboration and translational programs.
