How It Works

1. Ingest

Connect asset registry, inspection imagery, telemetry, maintenance logs, and weather context.

2. Model

Estimate failure probability distributions across asset families and forecast horizons.

3. Explain

Surface evidence trails behind each score so teams understand why risk is changing.

4. Act

Push ranked interventions to planning and field execution workflows.

Pilot Blueprint

90-day design partner pilot with measurable outcomes.

Scope

  • 1 to 2 asset families with clear failure modes
  • Historical failures, work orders, and inspection history
  • Multi-modal evidence bundle and operational constraints
  • Baseline benchmark against current heuristics or schedule-driven maintenance

Success Metrics

  • Probability calibration quality on out-of-sample events
  • Recall at top-K ranked inspections
  • Reduction in unplanned events in pilot population
  • Reduction in avoidable truck rolls and corrective maintenance spikes
